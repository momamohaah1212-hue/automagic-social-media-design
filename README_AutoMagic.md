# 🚗 AUTO MAGIC — Social Media Ads Design

![Auto Magic Banner](banner.jpg)

## 📋 Project Overview

Created a **comprehensive visual identity and social media advertising campaign** for **AUTO MAGIC**, a car dealership. The goal was to reflect **luxury, trust, and speed** through high-quality graphic design.

---

## 🎯 Key Deliverables

### 🏆 Logo Design
- Crafted a sleek, modern logo featuring a **golden car silhouette** on a dark background
- Reflects the brand's premium positioning in the automotive market

### 📱 Social Media Banners
- Designed high-conversion banners for **Facebook** and **Instagram**
- English & Arabic versions to target a wider audience
- Consistent premium feel across all formats (Square, Story, Cover)

### 🎨 Visual Identity
- Maintained a premium **Black & Gold** theme across all assets
- Typography and color palette chosen to convey luxury and trust

### 🎬 Video Editing
- Produced a dynamic short **promotional video** showcasing the showroom and car fleet
